WHO ARE WE?

We are a non-profit organization that hopes to empower girls through curricular supported workshops.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide hands-on peer mentorship to young girls between the ages 15-19 through discussion based workshops. We hope to create safe spaces for learning where young girls in Rwanda can contribute to current matters while actively participating to provide solutions.

OUR VISION

Our ultimate vision is to create an extensive network of young women in Rwanda who are aware of their innate potential and ability to affect positively their communities. Our vision is for our program to act as a catalyst for young girls to converse, providing them with a platform that could be used to spur new initiatives and collaboration.
